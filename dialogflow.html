Weather Shaman

This voice assistant is meant to provide current weather, near-future weather, the forecast, or weather extremes at a specified location.

Ask questions as if you were talking to a human, either via text or speech.

Be careful to spell words correctly; the AI usually cannot guess meanings from misspelled words.

Sample Objectives:

  • Find the current temperature for Boston.
  • Find the current cloud cover for London, England tomorrow at about 5pm.
  • Find when the lowest visibility will be in Santiago, Chile this week.
  • Find the highest temperature in New Delhi over the next two days.
  • Get the 5-day forecast for Seattle.

Some sample questions to ask (verbally or by typing):

  • What is the weather in Portland, Oregon?
  • How is the visibility in San Fransisco, California?
  • How is the snow in Nuuk, Greenland?
  • What is the temperature in New Delhi?
  • What is the cloud cover in London?
  • When will the temperature be the highest in Cairo, Egypt?
  • When will be the highest chance of rain in Sapporo, Japan?
  • What is the forecast for Cape Town?
  • What is the current pressure in 97141?
